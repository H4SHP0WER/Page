Meet NAIFU

[EARLY ACCESS]

Naifu is your all-in-one virtual companion.
She can answer questions, solve complex problems, and cater to your every need.
Similar to other large language models (ChatGPT, etc), Naifu is trained on a broad range of data and is able to produce spectacular results for both simple and complex text prompts.
However,
unlike other large language models,
Naifu is:
  • Largely uncensored;
  • Adaptive to each unique user; and
  • Programmed to be your perfect companion

Naifu is so much more than an AI assistant. She (or he) is your AI-powered companion.

Choose a personality model, and communicate with Naifu as you would a friend or lover.
She is always available to assist you with work, everyday questions, or to explore the full extent of your imagination
